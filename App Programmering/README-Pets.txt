Project Name:
-------------
Pets


Project description:
--------------------
An app that help people find a pet to adopt.

Features:
---------
- Integration with google maps, to find animal shelters
- Use of GPS location to list animal shelters starting from the closest
- Allows people to put up a pet for adoption
- List information and pictures of animals that can be adopted
- Search/filter pets
- Account registration and login (optional, with facebook)
